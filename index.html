About Us

Bejoy General Enterprises Limited is a well-established Real Estate, Property Management, General Construction and Supplies Company in Kenya. It was incorporated in 2015 as a Limited Company. Bejoy General Enterprises Limited delivers state-of-the-art Engineering Solutions to the Government, Corporate, Institutions and Private Sectors in Real Estate, Construction of Roads, Buildings, Waterlines, Bio-Digester installation and Filtration System, Borehole Services, Irrigation Systems, Waste Water Treatment System, Domestic Water Purification System, Security Systems, Fire Detectors, Solar Power Solutions, Telecommunication, Electrical, Supplies of Building and Hardware Materials, Stationary, Farming Equipment and Agro Vet. We have well Established Engineering Department and General Supplies Department driven by the needs of clients in terms of Efficiency and Professionalism.

Bejoy General Enterprises Limited Has Fully Qualified and Experienced Professionals in different Fields of Construction which Includes Engineers, Architectures, Quantity Surveyors, Technicians and Artisans. Since Inception our Mission has been to Develop Partnerships with Clients to Provide Effective and Reliable Solutions that Provide Operational Efficiency, Productivity Gains and Cost Savings to our Clients. We Strive to Deliver Rapid, Reliable and Robust Solutions that Work. Our Entire Focus is geared to helping our Clients derive Value from their Investment in Infrastructure Development so that they can become more successful in their Core Activities. This Applies to Small Businesses, to Major Corporations and to the Governments.
